Notify! Coins - NTC

Acquiring Coins

Notify Coins can be generated or acquired in the following ways
  1. By Mining Directly from your Client App
  2. By completing tasks in the Job Center
  3. By referring others to join the Community
  4. By funding through your local Banks
  5. By playing games at the Game Center
  6. By creating posts that attract engagements from others
  7. By interacting with posts boosted by others

Transferring Coins

Notify Coins can be transferred as follows
  1. Between friends as a Gift
  2. Between Client and Expert as Payment for Job Delivered

Converting Coins

Notify Coins can be converted to cash as follows
  1. By selling directly to Notify! Bank
  2. By selling to other Notifiers

Private Messaging

Sending Private Messages

Private Messages are allowed between two mutual followers or from a followed account to the follower
  • Messages that originate from a non-followed account to another account would not be delivered, and in such case, a notification would be sent to the intending recipient with options to Allow or Decline. However, the sender would not be notified of the eventual decision of the intending recipient. If Allowed, a channel would be created and this would be visible to both parties.

    • Life Cycle of Private Messages

    Private Messages are deleted automatically from our server the moment it has been delivered to the intending recipient.
  • In case of loss of records, Notify! holds no responsibility of retrieving such messages. In the light of this, users are advised to backup any records that would be of importance to them.

    • Deleting Chat History

    Chat histories are not deleted mutually. When you delete your chat history, you only lose your own copy of the record, but the recipient would have their own copy unless they delete it.

    Job Requests

    Creating Job Requests

    The minimum bounty that can be placed on any Job is NTC0.10.
    However, to attract more experts, you can decide to increase the bounty.
  • You can use your Community Bonus Coins or Acquired Coins to place a Job Request.

    • Deleting Job Requests

    User might deem it fit to delete any job requests they have already created if they do not want to continue receiving bidding or bargain for such jobs again.

    Extending Job Expiration

    User might decide to extend the job expiration stated on their job request upon creation to allow more experts find their job.

    Marking Job as Completed

    User must mark any job as completed once it has been delivered to avoid serving the job to other users.

    Payment for Job Delivered

    At the end of every service, user must be sure to transfer the bargained amount to the expert who delivered the service.
  • Any user who failed to comply with this rule would be banned from our community without further notices and would loose access to all of our services.

    • Profile Viewing

    Linking to Profile

    A tap on username would link to the Profile Page of the user. However, an exception is linking from the Inbox Page, where Profile Page could be linked to by simply tapping on the user Display Picture.
  • Note: Users with private accounts may not be viewable by non-followers of such accounts.

    • Reporting Posts

    Reporting Others

    Any posts may be reported if there is a strong evidence that such posts violate the rules and regulations, privacy policy or terms of use of "Notify!" or any other party which may relate directly, indirectly or otherwise with "our" community.

    Being Reported

    If any of the contents you shared with our community is flagged as "inappropriate" by any other members, you would be notified and asked to take proper action within a given time. If such action is not taken within the stipulated time, your account may be suspended, and this may lead to permanent forfeiture of membership should you exceed the grace period.

    Referral Bonus

    Acquiring Bonus NTC

    The community may decide to reward you for referring others to become members of our community. However, a user may only select ONE and ONLY ONE account as their referrer and this is not changeable, once it has been submitted.

    Anyone who is suspected to operate multiple accounts for this purpose would be suspended from the community and such user would lose all acquired or generated wallet balance.

    Community Bonus

    How to Use

    The community may decide to reward you from time to time based on your impact. This reward would come in form of Notify! Coins which is transferable and convertible and can be used as BOUNTY on Job Requests.

    Posts Management

    Saving Post

    You can save a Post to read or interact with at a later time. Such posts would be saved in your collections until you decide to remove them.

    Email Verification

    Importance

    In case of accidental loss of access to your account, your Email address or Mobile Phone number is required to initiate the recovery process. The recovery process is faster if your registered Email has been verified prior to the loss of access.

    Steps to Verification

    Upon submission of your Email address, a verification link would be sent to the address. Verifying your Email address is as simple as clicking on the link and open in any web browser.

    Account Verification

    Requesting for Account Verification

    Users may request for account verification by providing the required documents as are listed below:
    1. Student, International or Government Issued Identity Card
    2. Email address
    3. Phone Number
    Once submission is made for verification, all that the user has to do is wait for a response from us. A confirmation email would be sent upon successful application.
  • Note that submission of account for verification does not mean that the account would be verified.
  • To request for Account Verification, please visit your Account Settings Page.

    • Bank Details Verification

    Submission

    To be able to carry out any transaction, you must submit your Bank Details for verification. This would be verified against your registered name. A failed verification means no payment would be sent to such account.

    Transaction Tracking

    Failed Transaction

    Any case of failed transaction should be reported to support@notify.com.ng for immediate verification and rectification.

    Complaints and Suggestions

    How to make a submission

    You may send all your complaints and suggestions for improvements to support@notify.com.ng

    Bugs and fixes should be submitted to admin@notify.com.ng
